Why have multiple credit cards?¶

A lot of these premium credit cards have a cash value credit applied to them for the year to help normal customers justify the annual fee, but you’re not normal;). I think the AMEX DELTA skymiles RESERVE card is one of the best examples of this. It comes with $200 hotel credit, $240 RESY credit and one companion pass(this will work flying to hawaii which can be more than $2-3k flying first class). If you value the companion pass on the low end at $250, now all these benefits add up to $690 and for someone paying $650/year for the card you need to use all the benefits to justify the fee, but for you it’s completely free!

The benefits of credit cards stack on top of each other! For example, you can also have a DELTA platinum card and upgrade it to a DELTA reserve card and hold two of them. Now you have $1380 of value per year just from holding two cards! If I used these benefits for my ~10 year service commitment, I’ve now saved $13,800 from only two credit cards!
